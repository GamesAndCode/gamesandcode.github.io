Privacy policy of Office after hours

Effective Date: October 14, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect any data when you interact with our game. By playing our game, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect two types of data when you use our game:

Anonymous Data

We collect anonymous gameplay data to improve the overall game experience. This data helps us understand how players interact with the game and includes:

  • Gameplay events (e.g., level completion, game modes played)
  • Device information (e.g., operating system, device type)
  • Error reports (e.g., crash logs)

No personally identifiable information (such as name, email address, or location) is collected.

Voluntarily Provided Data

If you contact us for support or feedback, we may collect your email address or other information necessary to resolve the issue.

2. Why We Collect Data

The data we collect is used solely for the purpose of improving the game. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Enhance game features
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand player preferences and behaviors
  • Improve game performance and stability

3. How We Collect Data

We use Unity Analytics to collect and process anonymous gameplay data. Unity Analytics is a third-party tool that helps us analyze user behavior without identifying individual users. For more information on how Unity handles data, please review Unity’s Privacy Policy.

4. How We Protect Your Data

We take data security seriously. All collected data is anonymized and stored securely. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable laws like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You may request to see the data we collect about you.
  • Right to Rectify: You can ask us to correct any inaccurate information.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request the deletion of your data at any time.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can change your consent to data collection at any time via the game settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please use the ingame settings menu or contact us at support@gameandcode.com.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Unity Analytics, which may collect data in accordance with its own privacy practices. We encourage you to review Unity’s Privacy Policy for more details.

We do not share or sell any of your data to third parties, except when required by law.

7. Consent and Opt-Out

Upon first launching the game, you will be asked for your consent to collect anonymous gameplay data. You can choose to accept or decline. If you change your mind later, you can update your preferences in the game’s settings menu.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, or if you wish to exercise any of your rights, you can contact us at: Email: contact@gameandcode.com
