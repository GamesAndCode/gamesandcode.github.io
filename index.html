Office After Hours

A first-person psychological horror game where players uncover story-driven anomalies, while put on overtime by the boss. With over 40 anomalies and a balance of tension and humor, the game surprises players with moments that will either make them scream or laugh. At its core, Office After Hours explores the pressure of overwork and the relentless demand for perfect performance in modern corporate life.
